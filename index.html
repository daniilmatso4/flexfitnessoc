About Flex Fitness

Built for lifters, run with heart

Flex Fitness was created by founder Tony Nammari to raise the standard in Orange County. High standards, strong work ethic, and years of experience shaped a space that feels personal and delivers real results. Expect a clean, organized floor, staff who greet you by name, and the luxuries of a corporate gym, without the crowds or the cold vibe. This is a respectful community that takes progress seriously and treats every member with care.

“I built Flex Fitness to be the premier bodybuilding gym in Orange County. A place where standards stay high, members feel seen, and results speak for themselves.”
