How It Started

Born From the Grind

Flex started with a simple idea: create the gym we wished existed. No waiting for equipment. No cluttered layouts. No distractions. Just elite machines, a clean atmosphere, and a culture that values progress. Since opening in 2012, we’ve grown into a hub for bodybuilders, powerlifters, athletes, and everyday people who want results.

“I built Flex Fitness to be the premier bodybuilding gym in Orange County. A place where standards stay high, members feel seen, and results speak for themselves.”

Tony Nammari
Founder
